About Me

Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor at NIT Jalandhar in the Department of Mathematics & Computing. I hold a PhD in Optimization from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (2013). My expertise spans Swarm Intelligence, Evolutionary Computation, Artificial Intelligence, and Optimization techniques.
